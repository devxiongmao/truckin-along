# User Journey: Trucking Professional Sign Up Experience

## Persona

**Name:** Peter Parker, a person matching our standard trucking professional profile
**Role:** First time user who is affiliated with a trucking company
**Goal:** Wants to sign up for truckin' along to manage trucks, drivers and ship packages.

---

## Journey Overview

Ideally, we want word of mouth around the app to be positive, therefore this user would have heard about the app or discovered it through our marketing, and decided to check it out in order to take advantage of the app. They're here going through this journey because they believe they can get some value out of the app. They believe it'll help them be efficient, manage packages, and make some money.

---

## Steps

1. **Entry Point**

   - User goes to the root page of trucking along, sees the welcome screen and description of the roles, then clicks on the appropriate button
   - User navigates to the root homepage, and clicks on the `Sign Up as Trucking Professional` link

2. **Filling out the form**

   - Once the button is clicked, they're presented with a form. They'll be required to enter a First Name, Last Name, Email Address, drivers license and their password.
   - A sign up form will be filled out with 6 fields.

3. **Submitting the Form**

   - Once form is filled out, click the `Sign Up` button
   - If there are errors, user will be alerted immediately with all previous information still present.

4. **First link click**

   - Once successfully signed up, they're redirected to the homepage again with a banner at the top that says `Welcome! You have signed up successfully.`
   - Once they click any new link, they'll proceed to the next step

5. **Create Company**

   - Upon clicking any page within the app, they'll be prompted to create their company. This is another form that have two fields: Company Name, and Company Address
   - Users need to fill out this form, and click `Save Company`

6. **Create Company**

   - Upon clicking any page within the app, they'll be prompted to create their company. This is another form that have two fields: Company Name, and Company Address
   - Users need to fill out this form, and click `Save Company`

7. **Final Step**

   - At this point, they'll see whatever page they clicked on and are able to access all aspects of the app.

---

## Emotions

- 🟢 Happy with relatively easy sign-up
- 🟢 Lightweight sign up process without excessive cognitive strain
- 🟡 Confused that upon signup, they're redirected to the page that has the same `Sign Up` buttons. Not enough significant indications that they're now logged in.
- 🟡 Confused that upon signup, they need to still create their company before they can access the app.
- 🟡 `Save Company` verbiage is a little weird. Implies the existance that a user can have multiple companies, which is not true.

---

## Pain Points

- Slight confusion on redirection after signing up.
- Disjoint signup process related to company creation.

---

## Opportunities

- Upon login, content of the main page should change so that users are not confused regarding whether their now signed in/up correctly or not. (XMDEV-316)
- For trucking professionals, users should be prompted to create their company immediately after filling out their personal info. (XMDEV-319)

---

## Outcome

By the end of this journey, the user succeeded in creating their admin account along with their company profile
